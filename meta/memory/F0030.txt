Formalize the tile-surround constellation bound

Write down the low-30s upper bound for surrounding a hat tile in terms of true
internal vertices plus vertices that can appear between surrounding tiles.  This
is not the naive bound obtained by multiplying the maximum single-vertex
constellation by every vertex of the seed tile.  Adjacent vertex surrounds share
structure, so the relevant bound is topological and should be much smaller.

The working estimate is roughly 14 seed/internal vertices plus a small number of
possible between-tile vertices under maximal topological hexagonal or heptagonal
surround assumptions, giving a horizon around 32 to the mid-30s.  This should be
made precise and then checked against generated surround data.
