Extract RL1 tile-surround constellations

Use complete-enough RL0 completion data to surround an entire hat tile and emit
candidate RL1 tile-surround constellations.  Track the number of distinct
constellations, their canonical forms, and whether live-boundary pruning keeps
the dataset small.  This is the next step from single-vertex completions toward
runlevel data that describes whole-tile neighborhoods.

The extraction should preserve the differential model: RL0 supplies local
completion facts, but RL1 should record reproducible filesystem-backed data with
metadata about tile, lattice, max hidden level, live-only setting,
canonicalization version, source commands, and expected counts.  The output
should be suitable for later recomputation and comparison, not just one-off
experimentation.
